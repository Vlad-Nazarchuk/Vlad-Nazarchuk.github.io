Liubov Antonenko

«…have no fear, for I have taken up your cause; naming you by your name, I have made you mine. When you go through the waters, I will be with you; and through the rivers, they will not go over you: when you go through the fire, you will not be burned; and the flame will have no power over you» Isaiah 43:1-2 mother proclaiming during pregnancy because her unborn baby was on the verge of life and death, and during childbirth was born a cripple. She believed and prayed for the healing of the baby. The Lord saved her and healed the newborn girl. How do I know? That child is me! Now I´m 21 years old, absolutely healthy and can not imagine life without Jesus. Through stories about the Savior, since childhood there was a desire to serve Him.

Thank God for one day introduced me to the youth team InLight who love Jesus with all their heart. It's not just a team, it's a family. Here taught us to look at Jesus, not at the circumstances, without fear and doubt to take steps by faith. It is a great honour for me to be part of this family and serve to people.

In 18 years my life turned over on 180. Dad had gotten stomach cancer. I had grown in hours from a teenager into an adult independent woman. It was a difficult time of treatment dad, spiritual struggle for the salvation of his soul. From my lips didn't cease prayers, and the Bible did not leave my palms. Before he died, he accepted Jesus as his Savior. I praise Heavenly Father for his support in this difficult time, praise the Lord for making me a warrior and even then preparing for a new page of my life.

Once was important call from my spiritual mother Natasha. I've decided move to Spain. I knew that moving was God's volition and His plan! I lost my job 2 months before moving. There was a panic because my wallet was empty, but Jesus had everything under control. I didn't know how to leave my mother, because after everything of her health deteriorated.

The Bible says «The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want» (Psalm 23:1) and I believed Him, because the Lord is true to His Word. Now mother lives much better, than before, and health is improving. In their 51 she plans fly to me. Having arrived in Spain, God began to realize my dreams and continues to implement them now: I study at the University level courses, play volleyball with a professional coach and travel (while in Spain). But the most important thing for me is to be a servant in the mission "CaminoSanto", which is created by our strong family "InLight". This is a new beginning not only of my story, the mission of "CaminoSanto" will be a new beginning for everyone, because we believe in a Great God Who not real things - makes real!
