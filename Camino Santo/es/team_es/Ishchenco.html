Alexander and Natalia Ishchenko

We are Sasha + Natasha, two people in love with life, Jesus and each other)…

For 20 years now we have been married by heaven, walking hand by hand in marriage.

Many years as we are growing as parents: gave birth to two children, adopted two, accepted in the hearts of many spiritual... We are rich, very rich!

We have been ploughing the “fields” of service to God for 20 years: biblical class, Sunday school, women's group, theatrical service, the worship service of God, service in boarding schools, the youth club, the organization of camps, mentoring.

We have lived in Ukraine for almost 40 years of our life, trusting God with our lives and our own way, teaching others that God brought to the threshold of our lives. Spreading the Gospel, we weren't stopped by the hard circumstances of life, loss, deprivation, and the arrows of the enemy. They only became a challenge, to unite, to strengthen spiritual positions, to put on biblical equipment and with the power of Jesus – to attack and become winners! In every spiritual battle, in every life situation - we built God's Kingdom, glorifying and asserting His dominance and power on the Ukrainian land.

We heard a voice! ...As Abraham was called to come out of his native land, so for us, one day God opened new doors, tightly closing all the old ones that were dear and native. And this voice is so familiar, won´t confuse it with anything, the voice that drove us for many years, the voice that sounded and sounds calm, even in the darkest tornado of life. We listened, went, knowing little that awaits us ahead, only trust to the Lord´s voice…

This is the third year we are in Spain. The country that has now become our home because our homeland is in heaven and our home is where we are at the epicentre of God's will for us. We have experienced the passage through the desert here, in which God fed and watered, spoke and raised. He revealed secrets that were hidden in prophecies, visions and dreams that were given long before the "going out and entering"…

God entrusted us with a land that is beautiful and wonderful here. The land of which we prayed and professed by faith when we had nothing and didn´t see outlines of the "oasis" on the horizon. The land on which our dreams and goals have already begun to be realize. The land on which God began to assemble a team with one vision: to serve God, worshipping together, reaching the unsaved, revealing to them the secret of the happiness of life in the light of Jesus.

We invite you to share with us the joy of serving God in the “CaminoSanto” mission. In everyone who comes to us, we see someone who "climbed a tree to see the Master", who was rejected and ridiculed, who gives the last drops of his oil to bow to the feet of the Messiah, who was condemned and who is targeted by the stones, “The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because he hath anointed me to preach the gospel to the poor; he hath sent me to heal the brokenhearted, to preach deliverance to the captives, and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty them that are bruised, to preach the acceptable year of the Lord.” Luke 4:18-19
